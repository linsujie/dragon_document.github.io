Detailed Description

Main part of cparamlib; functions to calculate of differential cross sections.

File provides functions for the calculation of inclusive cross sections for stable secondary particles (gamma rays, electrons, positrons, electron neutrinos, electron antineutrinos, muon neutrinos and muon antineturinos) and transverse momentum distributions for gamma rays, as described in Kamae et al. (2006) and Karlsson and Kamae (2008). It also provides functions for the calculation of the inelastic proton-proton cross section given in Kamae et al. (2006).
