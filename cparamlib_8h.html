◆ antinue_param_delta()

void antinue_param_delta ( double  Tp,
PARAMSET *  params 
)

Calculates parameters c0,...,c4 describing the Delta(1232) electron antineutrino inclusive cross section as a function of the proton kinetic energy Tp. Since the negative charged Delta(1232) resonance is not formed, no electron antineutrinos are produced and thus this function only sets the PARAMSET::c array to zero.

Parameters
TpProton kinetic energy in GeV.
paramsPointer to a PARAMSET struct where the calculated parameters will be stored.
